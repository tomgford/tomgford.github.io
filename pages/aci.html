Assassins Creed: India

Back
  • Client: Ubisoft / Climax Studios / Engine House
  • Service: Lead Animator

Working with the talented guys at Engine House VFX I was fortunate to be one of the lead designers for the immensely popular Assassins Creed game franchise. My role entailed posing up the character in 3Ds Max, adding them into compositions and sending them off to an illustrator. Once the scenes were painted it was my job to animate each element to give the scenes depth and bring them to life. The final result is an eye catching set of cutscenes that match the style of the related games.
